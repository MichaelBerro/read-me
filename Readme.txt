Guide to use the program:

1. To Sign up: While on the home page click on login/sign up then click on Sign up button and enter your information and then click sign up to finish

2. To login: While on home page enter your username and password and click login

3. To Assign members: While on the dashboard page click next to the paper’s PDF file name on Assign members and then chose which members you want to assign

4. To insert paper: While on dashboard page click on the top blue button on insert New paper

5. To review the reviews of a paper:
	Click on Select next to each paper’s name
6. To view the accepted papers: click on All accepted papers
Papers" button

7. To insert new pc member: click on Insert New Pc members and then put the name and email address and click insert

8. To view pc members: click on Pc members on the dashboard page and you can view them one by one and you can update their information and delete them if required

9. to go back to dashboard: click on conference paper

10. To logout: click on Log out
